Who Are We?

Mediocre Electronics

Here at Mediocre Electronics we strive to make affordable and useable electronics available to the whole world regardless of ethnic, political or economical background. We strive to put thought and effort into all of our products which is why each product is hand made and tested before it is put on the market.

 

 

Mediocre Sound

Mediocre Sound was created to bring state of the art recording and listening music to everyone. We use rare earth magnets in all of our speakers and carefully tested acoustics for all of our speakers so that you can get the most out of your mediocre speakers.

 

Mediocre Reality

Although this is still in the beta stages Mediocre Reality is an augmented reality line that we intend to release by 2025. We use lcd screens with 4K resolution embedded in sunglasses for a discreet interaction with your computer. Our Mediocre Reality line is compatable with the Rasberry Pi and any computer with HDMI output that is running Mediocre OS.

Things to Keep in Mind

Mediocre Electronics promises that any products you order worked when we put them in the box. We also promise that all products were personally kissed by the founder of this company. We would also like you to keep in mind that we make each order individually and are currently in the process of building up inventory so please allow due time for products to be made and shipped.
